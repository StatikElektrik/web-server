What is PTAI ?

PTAI is an AI-based predictive maintenance optimization tool for company's operations. With PTAI, you can minimize downtime, reduce maintenance costs, and maximize the efficiency of your operations.
PTAI utilizes machine learning algorithms, IoT sensors, and predictive analytics to monitor the health of your vehicles and predict when maintenance is needed before a breakdown occurs.
